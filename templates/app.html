About


it is a chatbot which is trained on deep learning model. so far it has just 900 words in its dictionary which is the same like a three years old kid. it is trying to learn which word is the best for using in the context.

'Seq2Seq' deep learning model it is combination of encoder and decoder where encoder model takes questions as input and pass the final states to decoder model it takes states and starting word token to predict first word of the answer. on the basis of first word and updated states it predicts second word and it goes on until it gets the ending word token.
